About Me

Hi, I am Ken Leslie Guerrero Endrina, 22 years old, living in Lugdungan Dipolog City. I am currently studying at Jose Rizal Memorial State University (JRMSU), pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. I have 3 siblings: 1 girl and 2 boys. I am the second eldest among them. My father's name is Alexander, and my mother's name is Ma. Herminia.

My Perception in Life

Since childhood, I always believed that life would be better as I grow up, more adventurous and easier. However, as I've grown older, I've come to understand the challenges of adulthood. I never imagined how difficult life could be with numerous problems and struggles to face daily in the real world. I've gained insights into why some people have suicidal thoughts and feel lost. Despite the challenges, I reflect on my childhood days when I could be carefree and happy. Life goes on, and we must accept its realities.

The Lesson I've Learned from Life

The lesson that resonates with me and keeps me going is "Never Give Up. Just pray to God every time and think I am special, appreciated, and unique in my own way."
